About Doghustle

Let me explain how doghustle came to life by a small story:

One day a young man who was sick and tired of his day job wanted to shift gears and apply for startups. Being a programmer by specialization, he wanted to learn something new and started experimenting with programming languages.He slowly started coding small projects,which gradually increased in size. He was slowly increasing his skillset and day after day, his confidence started increasing. He had many demo projects whose source code was hosted at GitHub/Bitbucket. He was now ready to face the startup world.

He started applying to all jobs he found interesting and he was getting a good response too. But they all asked him his Linkedin Profile,Github Profile and his demo project's URL's.

He patiently started filling in the forms,giving all the social account details and explaining how 'XYZ' project was done and why it was not hosted yet.

He started spending more time updating his resume than doing what he loved doing - CODING !

Needless to say, he was frustrated. Then he wondered whether his fellow coding heroes faced the same problem he was facing.

And the rest as they say is History. This is the origin of Doghustle.

Please note that only the Github Integration has been done till now. Will work on the BitBucket integration as well.
